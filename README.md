# মনবন্ধু

### Community Health Navigator for Rural Bangladesh

### (CHNRB)

### A. Stress Checker

1. Checks stress daily using Bangla prompts and makes a longitude view(graph) od the
    users emotional state. It helps to relief from mental problems.
2. If a user opens the app after a long time the prompt should change and act as a friend
    meeting after a long time.

### B. Health Map

1. The webapp needs to have a map that shows healthcare centers near me and across the
    map.
2. It needs to be offline and online both
    for offline it need to have a local landmarks

## C. Anonymous Help

1. A private help seeking mechanism that allows user to consult about their mental health
    without revealing their identity.
2. The message should go to a NGO volunteer and it should be offline and online both.

## D. Seasonal Prevention

1. There should be a prompt someplace in the webapp about the season change and the
    most risky diseases in that season and have some ways to prevent them
2. Stop spreading fake WhatsApp messages and give practical ways to prevent diseases.

### E. Antenatal Care

1. Privacy-first tracker of antennal visit and child vaccination camp aligned with
    Bangladesh’s Expanded Program on Immunization (EPI)
2. Must function offline


## F. Symptoms awareness

1. Make users know about concerning symptoms and advice to use medical help instead of
    ghoroya chikitsha.
2. Give practical solutions and not encourage to self-dragonize from an app.

### G. Community Health Event

1. Make a calendar that shows upcoming medical bootcamps and events like blood
    donations drives free health screenings.
2. It should give notifications or prompts to notify everyone about upcoming events.

### H. Volunteers

1. A searchable directory of trained volunteers in different locations of villages. They can
    give health advice, mental first aid, maternal health, chronic disease.
2. They need to have a upazila wise section to contact them easily.

### I. Data export

1. Develop anonymous data export that exports data to a NGO like total check ins, most
    viewed health contents , facility search trends.

### J. AI

1. Build a voice assistant that can analyze peoples language and respond to them in the
    same language.
